The system itself

The Creighton Model FertilityCare™ System and the underlying clinical science were developed by Dr. Thomas W. Hilgers and the Pope Paul VI Institute for the Study of Human Reproduction. To find a certified practitioner anywhere in the world, visit FertilityCare.org.

Primary reference

Almost every clinical detail in this app — the observation codes, the stamping rules, the Peak Day definition, the post-Peak count, the Vaginal Discharge Recording System used by the observation form — comes from this textbook:

Hilgers, Thomas W. The Creighton Model FertilityCare™ System: An Authentic Language of a Woman's Health and Fertility. Pope Paul VI Institute Press, Omaha, Nebraska.
ISBN 978-0-9626485-4-0 / 0-9626485-4-X

If you want to learn the method properly, this is the book. We recommend reading it alongside instruction from a certified FertilityCare Practitioner; the book is the reference and the practitioner is the teacher.
